Swear Filter Bypasser

Here is a tool to convert Latin script texts to similar-looking characters in the Cherokee syllabary writing system. This can be useful for bypassing swear filters, or just giving your text a cool style.

Note: This tool currently only supports the 26 letters in the English alphabet. Other symbols will remain unaffected. Not all characters are converted, since some replacements couldn't be found.
